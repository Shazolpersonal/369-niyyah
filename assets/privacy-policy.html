369 Niyyah ("the App") is a daily Islamic affirmation app built to help users strengthen their connection with Allah through the 369 writing method. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

1. Data Collection

369 Niyyah does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The app operates entirely offline.

2. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

  • Your daily affirmation progress (which slots you have completed)
  • Your start date and streak count
  • Your language preference (English or Bengali)
  • Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third party.

3. Notifications

The app may request permission to send local push notifications to remind you of your daily affirmation sessions. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and are not powered by any external service. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

369 Niyyah does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with Google, Facebook, or any other company.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The content is suitable for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
